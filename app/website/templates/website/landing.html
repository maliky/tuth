Portal direction

Same visual language from student services to backstage operations.

The student portal is the reference for Tusis. Staff, finance, registrar, dean, and future roles inherit the same sidebar, header, cards, tables, badges, and action patterns.

  • Task-oriented sidebars replace admin-style navigation.
  • Role switching lives inside the sidebar workspace header.
  • Advanced admin links stay available but no longer drive the main UX.
